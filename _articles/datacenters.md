## Datacenter network architecture

- M. Al-Fares, et. al.
  [A scalable, commodity data center network architecture](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/1402946.1402967).
  _ACM SIGCOMM '08, August 2008_.
- A. Singh, et. al.
  [Jupiter Rising: A Decade of Clos Topologies and Centralized Control in Google's Datacenter Network](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/2829988.2787508).
  _ACM SIGCOMM Computer Communication Review, vol. 45, n. 4, August 2015_

## Congestion control in datacenters

- M. Alizadeh, et. al.
  [Data center TCP](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/1851182.1851192).
  _ACM SIGCOMM '10 conference, August 2010_
- W. Chen, et. al.
  [Comprehensive understanding of TCP Incast problem](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/7218549).
  _IEEE Infocom '15, April 2015_
- A. Dhamija, et. al.
  [A large-scale deployment of DCTCP](https://www.usenix.org/conference/nsdi24/presentation/dhamija).
  _USENIX NSDI '24 conference, Santa Clara, CA, USA, April 2024_

## Remote direct data access

- Y. Zhu, et. al.
  [Congestion Control for Large-Scale RDMA Deployments](https://conferences.sigcomm.org/sigcomm/2015/pdf/papers/p523.pdf).
  In ACM SIGCOMM '15, London, UK, August 2015.
- A. Gangidi, et. al.
  [RDMA over Ethernet for Distributed AI Training at Meta Scale](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3651890.3672233).
  In ACM SIGCOMM '24, Sydney, Australia, August 2024.
- J. Hu, et. al.
  [RDMA Transports in Datacenter Networks: Survey](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10521648).
  _IEEE Network, vol. 38, n. 6, November 2024_
